About ACTIN

ACTIN is a leading influencer marketing and casting agency with operations in Lucknow and Mumbai. Established in 2022, we specialize in connecting brands, filmmakers, and production houses with the right influencers, actors, and creative talent.

With a network of 5,000+ verified influencers and actors, ACTIN has become one of Lucknow's largest influencer marketing agencies, delivering impactful campaigns and professional casting solutions across India.

Our expertise extends beyond influencer marketing into film and television casting. Founded by Himanshu Mishra, an experienced casting director, ACTIN has collaborated with several renowned film and entertainment projects.

Whether you're a brand looking to create high-impact influencer campaigns or a production house searching for the perfect cast, ACTIN offers end-to-end talent solutions with professionalism, creativity, and reliability.
